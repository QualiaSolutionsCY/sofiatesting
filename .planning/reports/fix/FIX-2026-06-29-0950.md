# Fix Report - 2026-06-29 09:50

**Symptom:** Creating a monthly invoice via Sophia on WhatsApp, the agent gives the recipient email when Sophia asks — but on approval the invoice is NOT emailed to that address, and marios@zyprus.com is not CC'd. Operator expectation: approve → email the client, Marios CC'd by default.
**Mode:** quick (backend) — Sophia invoicing flow.
**Outcome:** fixed

## Root Cause
`lib/invoices/sophia/intent-handlers.ts` — `create_draft` (194-228) stores the captured email as `clientEmail` but sends nothing (reply: "Queued to Marios for approval"). The `approve` case (273-309) fired only `sendDocumentToAccountingGroup` + `notifyMariosApprovedAction` (both WhatsApp) and **never emailed the client**. The stored client email was only ever used by the separate `email_invoice` intent ("email this invoice to …"), so approving a Sophia-created recurring invoice delivered nothing to the client and didn't CC Marios.

## Patch (1 file)
- `lib/invoices/sophia/intent-handlers.ts` — in the `approve` case, after the group + Marios sends: if the invoice has a captured `clientEmail`, call `sendInvoiceEmailAction(updated.id, [clientEmail, "marios@zyprus.com"])` (best-effort; never fails the approve) so the client receives the invoice PDF and **marios@zyprus.com is always CC'd**. The reply now reports "emailed to {client} (cc marios@zyprus.com)".

## Verification
- `npx tsc --noEmit` — intent-handlers.ts PASS
- Live: operator to approve a Sophia-created invoice that has an email and confirm receipt (pending deploy)

## Remaining Notes
- Scope: auto-emails whenever an invoice has a captured client email (recurring or one-off-with-email); emailless invoices are unaffected. Marios CC'd always.
- Body uses sendInvoiceEmailAction's default ("Please find attached invoice N …"). Can be aligned to the panel's composed "Hello … on behalf of Marios … via Sophia" wording if desired.
