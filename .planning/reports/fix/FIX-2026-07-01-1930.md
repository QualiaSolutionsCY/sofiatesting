- B: `lib/invoices/email.ts` / `lib/invoices/actions/documents.ts` — the invoice email was sent as plain text only (no bold possible) and carried no "Sophia is AI" statement.
- C: `components/invoices/redesign/ledger/ListPane.tsx` sort — drafts-first + issue-date ordering, so the newest invoice (e.g. № 11479) wasn't at the top.
