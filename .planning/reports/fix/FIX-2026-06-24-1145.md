## Root Cause
- `lib/whatsapp/client.ts:271` + `supabase/functions/sophia-bot/utils/wasend.ts:604` — prior `caption ?? default` sent a TRULY-empty `text` for blank invoice captions; WaSender rejects empty text on a document send, so the group/Marios send failed.
- `lib/invoices/numbering.ts:32` — `getNextOfficialNumber` only advances past officials, while `getNextDraftSequence` advances past drafts+officials; with unapproved drafts present, approval handed back a lower next-official (11435) than the draft (11437).

## Patch
- `client.ts` / `wasend.ts`: blank caption -> single space `" "` (renders blank, send succeeds).
- `numbering.ts`: new `officialNumberOnApproval()` carries the draft's own sequence to the official (with collision guard).
- `documents.ts`: `approveDocumentAction` uses `officialNumberOnApproval`.
