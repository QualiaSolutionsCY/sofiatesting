**Symptom:** Cancelling an invoice with a typed cancellation reason — the accounting group still gets the default "Credit note N regarding the cancellation of invoice M", not the operator's reason.
**Mode:** quick (backend). **Outcome:** fixed

`lib/invoices/actions/documents.ts` — `cancelWithCreditNoteAction` computes `trimmedReason` (line 524) but calls `notifyGroupOfCreditNote(invoice, creditNote)` (line 548) WITHOUT it; that helper hardcoded the group caption (line 569-570) and deliberately discarded the reason ("the cancellation reason is NEVER included"). Same pattern as the correction-resend bug.

## Patch (1 file)
- `documents.ts` — pass `trimmedReason` into `notifyGroupOfCreditNote` (new optional `reason` param); the caption is now the typed reason when present, else the default credit-note notice. Reason still never rendered on the PDF/preview.
