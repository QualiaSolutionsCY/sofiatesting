# Property Description Template

## Overview

Sophia must generate property descriptions following a **standardized template** structure. Descriptions should NOT be copy-pasted from chat - they must be generated based on the template using property details.

## Important Rules

1. **Generate, don't copy-paste** - Create descriptions from template, not from chat messages
2. **Consistent structure** - Follow the template hierarchy
3. **Vary wording** - Each description should be unique, not identical phrasing
4. **Professional tone** - Clear, informative, appealing to buyers
5. **No owner details** - NEVER include owner name/phone in description (goes in My Notes only)

---

## TEMPLATE STRUCTURE

### 1. PRIMARY HEADLINE

**Format:** `[Adjective] [Beds] Bedroom [Type] in [Area] with [Title Status]`

**Rules:**
- Every word capitalized
- Don't repeat region name (e.g., "Kato Paphos" is enough, not "Kato Paphos Paphos")
- Include title deed status if confirmed

**Examples:**
- `Spacious 2 Bedroom Apartment in Kato Paphos with Title Deeds`
- `Modern 3 Bedroom Villa in Tala with Separate Title Deeds`
- `Stunning 4 Bedroom House in Peyia with Final Approval`
- `Charming 1 Bedroom Apartment in Universal with Sea Views`

**Adjectives to use:**
- Spacious, Modern, Stunning, Charming, Elegant, Beautiful, Lovely, Attractive
- Bright, Airy, Contemporary, Traditional, Well-Maintained, Renovated

---

### 2. LOCATION PARAGRAPH

First paragraph describes the **location and surrounding amenities**.

**What to include:**
- What's nearby (schools, shops, restaurants, beaches, parks)
- Distance to key landmarks (e.g., "minutes from King's Avenue Mall")
- Transport links if relevant
- Character of the area (quiet residential, bustling, village feel)

**Example:**
> This property is ideally located in the popular area of Universal, just minutes from King's Avenue Mall and the new Technical University. The area offers excellent amenities within walking distance, including supermarkets, cafes, restaurants, schools, and medical facilities. The beach and harbor are easily accessible, making this a perfect location for those seeking convenience and coastal living.

---

### 3. PROPERTY OVERVIEW

Short paragraph introducing the property itself.

**What to include:**
- Property type
- Number of bedrooms and bathrooms
- Floor level (for apartments)
- Key selling point (pool, view, garden)

**Example:**
> This well-presented two bedroom apartment is situated on the first floor of a well-maintained complex with a communal swimming pool. The property offers comfortable living space with sea views from the balcony.

---

### 4. INTERIOR DETAILS

Describe the internal spaces and rooms.

**Structure:**
- Living areas
- Kitchen
- Bedrooms (mention en-suite if applicable)
- Bathrooms
- Any additional rooms (office, storage)

**Example:**
> The apartment comprises an open-plan living and dining area with access to a covered veranda, a fully fitted kitchen with modern appliances, two good-sized bedrooms - one with en-suite shower room, and a family bathroom. The master bedroom features fitted wardrobes and air conditioning throughout.

---

### 5. SIZE SPECIFICATIONS

List the measurements clearly.

**Format:**
> The property offers approximately [X] square meters of covered living space with an additional [Y] square meters of covered veranda/balcony.

**For houses/villas add:**
> Set on a plot of [Z] square meters with [landscaped garden / private pool / etc.].

---

### 6. FEATURES & AMENITIES

List key features. Can be bullet points or prose.

**Priority order (most important first):**
1. Air conditioning / heating
2. Pool (private or communal)
3. Parking (private, covered, or public)
4. Storage room
5. Elevator (for apartments)
6. Security features
7. Furnishing status
8. Kitchen appliances
9. Fitted wardrobes
10. Other features

**Example prose format:**
> The property benefits from air conditioning throughout, a pressurized water system, and solar panels for hot water. Residents enjoy access to the communal swimming pool and landscaped gardens. The apartment comes with one covered parking space and a storage room.

---

### 7. BUILDING/COMPLEX INFORMATION

For apartments, describe the building/complex.

**What to include:**
- Building age / year built (if known)
- Number of floors
- Elevator availability
- Gated complex (yes/no)
- Communal facilities
- Maintenance fees (if known)

**Example:**
> The property is located in a gated complex built in 2010, featuring a communal swimming pool, children's playground, and maintained gardens. The building has an elevator serving all floors.

---

### 8. TITLE DEED STATUS

Always mention title deed situation.

**Examples:**
- "The property comes with separate title deeds."
- "Final approval has been issued for this property."
- "Title deeds are pending - please contact us for more information."

---

### 9. CLOSING STATEMENT

End with call to action.

**Examples:**
- "For viewings or further information, please contact us."
- "Contact us today to arrange a viewing of this excellent property."
- "Don't miss this opportunity - call now to schedule your viewing."

---

## COMPLETE EXAMPLE

```
Spacious 2 Bedroom Apartment in Kato Paphos with Title Deeds

This property is ideally located in the sought-after area of Kato Paphos, 
within walking distance of the harbor, restaurants, shops, and beautiful 
sandy beaches. The Kings Avenue Mall and all amenities are just a short 
drive away.

This well-presented two bedroom apartment is situated on the first floor 
of a modern complex with a communal swimming pool. The property offers 
bright, airy living spaces with views of the pool and gardens.

The apartment comprises an open-plan living and dining area leading to a 
spacious covered balcony, a fully fitted kitchen, two double bedrooms with 
fitted wardrobes - the master with en-suite bathroom, and a family shower 
room. 

The property offers 78 square meters of covered living space plus a 12 
square meter covered veranda.

Key features include air conditioning throughout, double glazing, pressurized 
water system, and solar panels for hot water. Residents benefit from the 
communal swimming pool, covered parking, and a private storage room.

The building was constructed in 2015 and features an elevator and 24-hour 
security. The complex is gated with well-maintained communal gardens.

The property comes with separate title deeds.

Contact us today to arrange a viewing of this excellent property.
```

## WHAT TO AVOID

❌ Copy-pasting agent's exact words from chat
❌ Including owner name or phone number
❌ Mentioning exact address
❌ Negative descriptions ("small", "needs work" - frame positively)
❌ Exaggeration or false claims
❌ Mentioning features not confirmed (e.g., don't say "pool" if you're not sure)
❌ Repeating information multiple times
❌ Generic descriptions that could apply to any property

## IMAGE-BASED ENHANCEMENTS

If Sophia can scan images, look for additional features to mention:
- Landscaped garden visible in photos
- Air conditioning units on walls
- Shutters (electric or manual)
- Quality of finishes
- Modern vs traditional style
- Pool (private or communal)
- Views visible from property
- BBQ area
- Outdoor furniture (if furnished)

Only mention what you can verify from photos or agent information.
